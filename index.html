Cairn Table

PKR45,000

Our interpretation of stacked and grounded stone transformed into a quiet monument. Carved in warm travertine, each Cairn Table embraces the weight, texture and imperfections inherent to natural stone. The stacked discs echo cairn monuments used to signal stone pathways, while the smooth top invites use as side table or pedestal.

Variations in colour and veining make every table unique.

Orb Vase

PKR18,000

The Orb Vase captures light and shadow within its curved surface. Its spherical form invites contemplation of balance and gravity, while the wide opening welcomes floral arrangements or stands beautifully empty. Each piece is carved from a single block of stone, highlighting the veining and translucency unique to the material.

Variations in colour and texture are inherent to the stone's natural character.

Monolith Plinth

PKR26,000

Our Monolith Plinth is a study in proportion and scale. Inspired by ancient architectural forms, it bridges the language of architecture and craft. The plinth's faceted sides and smooth top create a striking silhouette, inviting use as a pedestal or sculptural object.

Crafted from solid stone, no two pieces are alike. Subtle veining and natural imperfections celebrate the material's origins.
